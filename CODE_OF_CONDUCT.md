# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Tinct is committed to providing a friendly, safe, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience level, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

Participants in this community are expected to:

- Be respectful in language and tone.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Give and receive feedback constructively.
- Focus discussions on ideas and behavior, not personal attacks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
- Personal insults, trolling, or sustained disruption of discussions.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Sexualized language or imagery in community spaces.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other project-managed communication channels.

## Reporting and Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. Contact the maintainer through the contact method listed on the maintainer profile: https://github.com/asher
2. If you cannot find a private contact method, open an issue requesting private follow-up without posting sensitive details.

All reports are reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as possible.

Possible enforcement actions include warning, temporary ban, or permanent ban, based on severity and repeated behavior.
